# 📈 Industrial Sales Strategy

### Lead Qualification (BANT)
* **Budget:** Is the project funded?
* **Authority:** Who is the Decision Maker (DM) vs. Influencer?
* **Need:** What is the technical pain point?
* **Timeline:** When is the PO (Purchase Order) expected?

### Follow-up Schedule
* **Day 0:** Quotation submission.
* **Day 3:** Technical clarification.
* **Day 7:** Commercial negotiation/Price justification.
