# 📋 MGPS Compliance & Audit Readiness Matrix
**Standards:** HTM 02-01 | JCI Facility Management & Safety (FMS) | CBAHI

This framework outlines the mandatory testing and validation protocols required to maintain "Full Notch" accreditation status for a 300+ bed medical facility.

---

### 🕒 1. Daily & Weekly Operational Checks (Shift In-Charge Level)
* **VIE/LOX Inventory:** Daily verification of tank levels vs. hospital occupancy.
* **Plant Room Integrity:** Weekly testing of the "Auto-Changeover" manifold switch.
* **Moisture Check:** Constant monitoring of Medical Air Dew Point (Target: -40°C).

### 📅 2. Quarterly Preventive Maintenance (PPM)
* **Gas Purity Analysis:** Sampling and testing for:
    * Particulate matter (Filters)
    * Oil Carryover (< 0.1 mg/m³)
    * Carbon Monoxide (< 5 ppm) and Carbon Dioxide (< 500 ppm).
* **Zonal Isolation (AVSU):** Physical exercise of zone valves to ensure zero-leakage during isolation.

### 🗓️ 3. Annual Validation & Verification (V&V)
* **Gas Specificity Test:** A "Cross-Connection" check to ensure only the intended gas is delivered to each specific terminal unit.
* **Pressure Drop Test:** Static and dynamic pressure tests to ensure 4 bar (60 psi) is maintained at the furthest bed during peak demand.
* **Alarm Calibration:** Master and Area alarm recalibration for pressure thresholds (Low/High).

### 🛡️ 4. Emergency & Risk Management
* **LOX Failure Protocol:** Step-by-step transition to the Backup Cylinder Manifold.
* **Power Failure Logic:** Verification that MGPS Alarms and Medical Air Compressors are on the **Essential/Emergency Power Grid (Generator & UPS)**.
