## 🚀 Overview
This repository contains the digitized operational framework for a **300+ bed high-acuity medical facility**. It is designed to manage the end-to-end journey of life-critical utilities—from the source (LOX/Plant) to the patient bed—ensuring zero downtime in high-stakes environments.

---

## 🛠 Technical Core

### **1. Cryogenic Management**
* **VIE (Vacuum Insulated Evaporator):** Managing Liquid Oxygen (LOX) expansion, vaporization rates, and telemetry-based monitoring.
* **Expansion Logic:** Precise calculation of liquid-to-gas conversion ratios (1:860) to ensure accurate supply forecasting.

### **2. Medical Air & Vacuum Systems**
* **Air Integrity:** Maintaining a **-40°C Dew Point** to prevent moisture in the pipeline, adhering to Pharmacopoeia standards.
* **Vacuum Reliability:** Ensuring a consistent **-600mmHg** at the plant source for critical surgical and ICU suction.

### **3. Compliance & Standards**
All frameworks and automation scripts in this folder are aligned with global healthcare standards:
* **HTM 02-01** (Health Technical Memorandum)
* **JCI** (Joint Commission International)
* **CBAHI** (Saudi Central Board for Accreditation of Healthcare Institutions)
