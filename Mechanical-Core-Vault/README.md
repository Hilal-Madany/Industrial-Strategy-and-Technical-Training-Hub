## 📐 The "System-First" Approach
I mentor students and engineers to look at the **System Flow** before the individual component. 



### **Engineering Logic Applied:**
1. **Load Calculation:** Whether it is Thermal (BTU) or Structural (Metric Tons).
2. **Selection of Standards:** Aligning with **IS 2062** for India or **ASTM/ASHRAE** for Gulf projects.
3. **BOM to BOQ Transition:** Translating a technical part list into a commercially viable tender document.
4. **Make-List Approval:** Selecting the right OEM (Nortek, Pall, Trane) for long-term **OPEX** reduction.
