## 🛠️ 1. The 3-Tier Maintenance Strategy
Effective FM is not about "fixing breaks"—it is about **Asset Lifecycle Management**. I implement a 3-tier approach to ensure 100% uptime:

| Tier | Category | Methodology | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | **Daily/Routine** | Shift-wise visual/audio inspections & pressure logging. | Early Detection |
| **Tier 2** | **Preventive (PPM)** | Scheduled filter, belt, and lubrication cycles. | Wear Mitigation |
| **Tier 3** | **Predictive** | Thermal imaging, vibration analysis, and Nitrogen integrity tests. | Zero Downtime |

## 🏥 2. Critical Infrastructure: MGPS & Utility Flow
In healthcare EPC, the **Medical Gas Pipeline System (MGPS)** is the most critical mechanical asset. My O&M manuals cover the entire flow from source to terminal.



### **Core Competencies in this Module:**
* **Source Management:** PSA Plant efficiency, LMO cryogenic tank safety, and Cylinder Manifold changeover logic.
* **Distribution Integrity:** ASTM B819 Copper piping standards and Degreasing/Purging discipline.
* **Monitoring:** BMS (Building Management System) integration for real-time alarm responses.
